QUESTION 2: Why must a SOC analyst NEVER use "Bridged" mode when detonating unknown malware?
ANSWER: "Bridged" mode connects the VM to the LAN. This leaves the host machine vulnerable 
because the malware will have unrestricted direct access to the local device and its unique IP address.
